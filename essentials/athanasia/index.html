About

DabAZ is an artist, musician, singer and producer in music. More than this, he is also an ethical hacker, designer and front-end developer. Raised in China and United States, he was introduced to pop, rock and hip-hop music at a young age. He had started his music career as a DJ in the band of his high school before he began producing his own songs and beats. Inspired by Lil Pump and Juice WRLD, he put more effort in making hip-hop music in 2018. Now, DabAZ makes different genres of music, including EDM, hip-hop, lo-fi, epic and rock.
